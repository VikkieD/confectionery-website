About Us

Beau’s Bonbons Store is a family owned business based in Ottawa. Triplets Sarah, Emily & Jane Beau built the business with the help of their relatives and it soon became a city wide business, having two stores in the capital area. We sell a variety of hand made candies and chocolates that you will not find at your local grocery store.

Our focus is on family and community which is why we insist on providing great customer service. When you buy from us you become part of our extended family and we truly care that we provide you edible happiness.
